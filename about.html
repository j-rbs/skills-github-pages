About Rubin Cyber

Hi, I’m Julia Rubinstein — a cybersecurity consultant based in Melbourne, helping small and mid-sized businesses build practical, sustainable security foundations.

My background is in cybersecurity operations and risk management, working with organizations that range from startups finding their footing to established teams modernizing their approach. I started Rubin Cyber to bring that expertise to businesses that don’t need enterprise-sized budgets — just clear guidance, realistic roadmaps, and someone who actually listens.

My philosophy is simple: security should enable growth, not get in its way. Whether a company is scaling, entering new markets, or formalizing governance, my focus is always on aligning cybersecurity with the business’s real goals — not just compliance checklists.

When I’m not working with clients, you’ll usually find me digging into new tools, writing about security trends, or exploring Melbourne’s coffee scene.
